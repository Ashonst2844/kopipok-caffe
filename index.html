Our Journey

2024

Realizing that coffee and tea are very popular beverages for everyone, especially teenagers and adults, we strive to create a menu that is the right choice, offering high quality and affordable prices.

2025

Opening the first KOPIPOK Jakarta Café in the capital city, Central Jakarta, Indonesia, with a variety of coffee and tea menus, as well as adding bakery items that are perfect to enjoy with our coffee and tea.

2026

Opened three branches in various cities in Indonesia: KOPIPOK Semarang Café in Semarang (Central Java), KOPIPOK Lampung Café in Bandar Lampung (Lampung), and KOPIPOK Surabaya Café in Surabaya (East Java), with additional menus tailored to each region.

2027

Will opened few stalls in mall in few cities like FX Sudirman, Grand Indonesia, Green Pramuka Square in Jakarta. Sumarecon Mall in Tangerang.And Rita Mall in Purwokerto.
